# Website Services Agreement (Draft)

**Parties**  
- **Service Provider:** Kresge Carpentry Web Partner (“Developer”)  
- **Client:** Kresge Carpentry LLC (“Client”)

**Project Overview**  
Developer will design, build, and launch a custom single-page marketing website showcasing Client’s carpentry services, process, testimonials, and contact information, based on the scope outlined in the accompanying proposal.

---

## 1. Scope of Work
| Phase | Deliverables | Fee |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Strategy & Content Alignment | Kickoff call, content audit, copywriting (2 revision rounds) | $750 |
| Design System & Layout | Responsive mockups (desktop + mobile), palette/typography/system | $1,100 |
| Front-End Build | Semantic HTML/CSS/JS, responsive components, mobile nav, animations | $1,650 |
| Asset Prep & Performance | Image sourcing, WebP/JPEG creation, lazy loading, Lighthouse tuning | $450 |
| SEO & Schema | Meta tags, canonical, OpenGraph/Twitter, LocalBusiness + FAQ schema | $350 |
| Contact & Lead Capture | Contact form markup, mailto/tel fallbacks, integration with form provider | $400 |
| Deployment & Handoff | Hosting setup, DNS/domain support, QA, README + update instructions | $600 |

**Subtotal:** $5,300  
**Optional Add-ons:**  
- Sitemap + robots.txt generation ($150)  
- Monthly support/updates (4 hrs/mo) ($360/mo or equivalent trade)  
- Quarterly content refresh (new photos/copy) ($450 per refresh)

> *Work-for-work option:* Fees can be exchanged for equivalent carpentry labor or materials up to the same value, to be scheduled mutually.

---

## 2. Timeline (Estimated)
- Week 1: Kickoff, content alignment, initial copy drafts.  
- Week 2: Design system + hi-fi mockups.  
- Weeks 3–4: Front-end build, asset optimization, SEO/schema.  
- Week 5: QA, deployment, domain connection, training/handoff.  
Timeline may shift based on revision cycles and content delivery.

---

## 3. Payment / Trade Terms
- 50% ($2,650) due at project start (or equivalent carpentry labor/materials).  
- 25% ($1,325) due upon approval of design mockups.  
- 25% ($1,325) due upon launch/handoff.  
- Add-ons billed separately upon approval. Late payments accrue 2% monthly interest after 15 days.

---

## 4. Client Responsibilities
- Provide timely feedback, logo files, service details, and approvals within 3 business days.  
- Supply accurate contact information (phone, email, licensing statements).  
- Maintain hosting, domain, and any third-party accounts after launch unless an ongoing support plan is selected.

---

## 5. Revisions & Change Requests
- Each phase includes up to two rounds of feedback. Additional revisions or new features will be quoted at $110/hr or corresponding trade rate.  
- Scope changes must be documented via email and approved before work proceeds.

---

## 6. Intellectual Property & Credits
- Upon full payment, Client owns the compiled site files and custom assets.  
- Developer retains the right to showcase the project in portfolios unless Client opts out in writing.

---

## 7. Warranty & Support
- Developer will fix launch-related bugs reported within 30 days at no charge.  
- Ongoing maintenance, content updates, or additional features require a support plan or hourly/trade agreement.

---

## 8. Termination
- If either party terminates before completion, Client pays for work performed to date; any unearned prepaid funds are refunded.  
- Either party may terminate with 7 days’ written notice if the other party materially breaches this agreement.
